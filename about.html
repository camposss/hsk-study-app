Hello, my name is Christian Campos and I am the creator of this app. The purpose of this app is to expose the user to as many characters as possible from each of the 6 levels of the HSK.

Each of these word lists were taken from data.hsk.hsk.com and are in no way meant to be used as commercial purposes. This is strictly for the love of studying Chinese characters!

Thank you for visiting my app, I appreciate the support and will continue to make HSK Study Cards better.
